# Incident Response Process

### 1️⃣ Preparation
Create policies, tools, and training.

### 2️⃣ Identification
Detect and confirm the incident.

### 3️⃣ Containment
Limit the damage.

### 4️⃣ Eradication
Remove the threat.

### 5️⃣ Recovery
Restore systems safely.

### 6️⃣ Lessons Learned
Document and improve defenses.
